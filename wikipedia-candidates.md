# Wikipedia Article Candidates

Pages that Natural Breadcrumbs links to with 🅦 but that don't exist on Wikipedia (or are stubs). Each is a potential article to write.

## Missing

- **Markov category** — Fritz 2020, 200+ citations. No article. nLab has a page. The foundational concept for categorical probability.
- **Imperative category** — Staton 2025. No article. "Traced distributive copy-discard category" — the structure that makes Hoare rules theorems.
- **Graded Hoare logic** — Gaboardi 2021. No article. Hoare triples parameterized by a preordered monoid grade.
- **Compositional game theory** — Hedges 2018. No article. Open games, Nash equilibrium as compositional predicate.
- **Categorical cybernetics** — Capucci 2021. No article. Selection relations on parametrised optics.
- **Possibilistic shadow** — Fritz-Perrone-Rezagholi 2021. No article. Support as monad morphism from valuations to Hoare hyperspace.

## Stubs or could be improved

- **Effectus theory** — Cho-Jacobs 2015. No article (redirects to "effect algebra" which is different).
- **Bisimulation metric** — Panangaden 2009. The "bisimulation" article exists but doesn't cover the metric/quantitative version.
- **Bayesian lens** — Smithe 2021. No article. The "lens (computer science)" article doesn't cover the Bayesian/statistical case.
